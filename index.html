Vision of Gram Panchayat

Our vision is to ensure transparent governance, improved infrastructure, clean drinking water, digital grievance redressal, and sustainable development for every citizen of Wandhali. We aim to empower villagers through participatory decision-making and modern technology.

Development Priorities

  • Road & Drainage Improvement
  • Water Supply Management
  • Swachh Bharat Implementation
  • Women Self-Help Group Support
  • Youth Skill Development Programs
