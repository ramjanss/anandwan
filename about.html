About Gram Panchayat Wandhali

Village Introduction

Gram Panchayat Wandhali is situated in Taluka Warora, District Chandrapur, Maharashtra. The village is known for its agricultural activities and active local governance system. The Gram Panchayat works towards rural development, infrastructure improvement, and digital governance for transparent administration.

History

The village has a rich cultural and historical background rooted in traditional Maharashtrian rural heritage. Over the years, Wandhali has developed through community participation and government development schemes.
